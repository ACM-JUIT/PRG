ACM Student Chapter, JUIT

This is the website for the Research Paper Reading Group at JUIT Waknaghat. People submit research papers they wish to read/find interesting on the group each week along with a short description of the paper. The session usually comprises of a short talk (roughly 20 minutes) accompanied by slides to give a general gist of the paper and the algorithm/technique without going into too many technicalities.
To ensure a steady stream of speakers, every person in the group has to present a paper during the course of a semester. After each meeting, the speakers for the next week will be decided alongwith a standby speaker incase of last minute problems. The posts for each meeting will be put on the ACM JUIT : The ACM Student Chapter, JUIT Waknaghat and our mailing list.
The group is open to all. You can join here.
We meet every week on Friday, at 6:30PM in CR 5/6.
The meetings have been stopped due to the end semester exams. We will be continuing from next semester onwards.
